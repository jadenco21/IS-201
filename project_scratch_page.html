My Heritage

Knowing where I came from is almost as important as where I plan to go.


    The Culture I Derive From:
  1. Samoa
    • My grandfather immigrated to Hawaii from Samoa as a teenager
    • Spending time in Hawaii where my mom grew up is where I feel closest to my roots
    • Learning how to cultivate indigenous plants and learning every use of the coconut are some of my favorite memories
  2. China
    • Seeing as my grandfather never met his Chinese father due to world conflict, I am driven to explore this side of me
    • I am now the only member of my family to speak the Mandarin Chinese Language
    • It is one of my life goals to reconnect my family to our lost Chinese geneology
  3. Utah
    • Growing up in small town in middle-of-nowhere Utah, my dad's self-motivated vision has become a family staple of high expectation
    • A self-made entrepreneur and business owner, my dad has taught me the value of grit and perseverance
    • My dad taught me drive is worthless without family and faith as primary motivators
